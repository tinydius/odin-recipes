Strawberry Cookies!

Description

Strawberry Cookies are a fun,festive, and SUPER SIMPLE way to add some pizazz to the table. The Strawberry cake mix with the white chocolate combination is UNDEFEATED.

Ingredients

  • 1 box Strawberry Cake mix
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 1 cup white chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F. Line 2 large baking sheets with parchment paper and set aside.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the cake mix and baking powder. combined.
  3. In a separate smaller bowl, whisk the eggs, oil, and vanilla together by hand. Whisk until well. Pour the egg mixture into the bowl with cake mix and stir with a spoon until a dough forms. Be sure to fully combine. Mix in the white chocolate chips.
  4. Using a small portion scoop, drop rounded balls of the dough onto your baking sheet. These cookies flatten out quite a bit, so make sure the balls are taller than they are wide, and give them quite a bit of space between each cookie.
  5. Bake for 9-10 minutes. Do not let them brown in the oven. They will appear slightly undercooked, but that is a good thing. They will firm up and flatten as they cool. When you take them out, you can add a couple more white chocolate chips to the top to make them look pretty
  6. Allow to cool for at least 10 minutes
