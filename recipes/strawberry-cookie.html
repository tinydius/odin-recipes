Strawberry Cookies!

Description

Strawberry Cookies are a fun,festive, and SUPER SIMPLE way to add some pizazz to the table. The Strawberry cake mix with the white chocolate combination is UNDEFEATED.

Ingredients

  • 1 box Strawberry Cake mix
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 1 cup white chocolate chips
