Cinnamon Rolls

Description

The BEST cinnamon rolls in the WORLD. Big,fluffy,soft, and undeniably delicious. No other recipe can compare, this recipe includes options to make them overnight or ahead of time and even freeze them.

Ingredients

  • 1 qt. whole milk
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 0.25 oz packets active dry yeast
  • 8 cups (plus 1 cup extra, reserved) all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp. (heaping) baking powder
  • 1 tsp. (scant) baking soda
  • 1 tbsp. (heaping) salt
  • Plenty of melted butter
  • 2 cups sugar
  • Generous sprinkling of cinnamon
